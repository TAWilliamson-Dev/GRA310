Welcome to Nebula Coffee!

Nebula Coffee Company provides an otherworldly coffee experience that's out of this world. We source only the highest-quality beans from across the galaxy, roast them in small batches to perfection, and use locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. Our mission is to create a sustainable space where our community can explore new flavors, make new connections, and embrace their inner explorer.
